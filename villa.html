8 ROOMS AND 3 APARTMENTS IN THIS IDYLLIC  STYLISH AND FAMILY OWNED VILLA AND VIA RENON 11 IN THE CENTRE OF BOLZANO.

Villa Anita Rooms is situated in a green environment of Bozen, and just 5 minutes walk  away from the city centre, close to the cable cars from Jenesien and Ritten and the train station.

 

The tastefully decorated rooms with parquet floors are equipped with a WiFi wireless Internet connection, shared bathrooms and some of them with balcony or terrace facing garden.

 

Enjoy the free bike rental service..
